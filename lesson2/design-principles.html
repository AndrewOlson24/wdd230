Visual hierarchy is the principle of arranging elements to show their order of importance. I believe this picture of Apples watches clearly shows an amazing picture and lets your eyes know that that is the importain thing on the web page.

White Space and Clean Design

The white space rule in design recommends using enough blank spaces in a design layout. The Google home page on both moblie and desktop shows basically the only option is to use the google feature. The search bar is the only focus of the page.
